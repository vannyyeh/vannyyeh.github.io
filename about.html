About Me

[i]

Hi, I am a multidisciplinary designer and developer who enjoys turning abstract ideas into practical, usable experiences. I began my career in interior and architectural design, where every concept had to be grounded in structure, constraints, and real world use. That foundation taught me to think in systems, balance creativity with feasibility, and design with intention.

As my interests shifted toward digital products, I moved into UI and UX design to explore how the same principles apply in interactive experiences. With training in architecture and human computer interaction, I approach design through user behavior, clarity, and structure. I am drawn to complex problems and enjoy shaping them into experiences that feel intuitive and purposeful.

Today, I work across research, design, and front end development, using AI driven workflows to move quickly from concept to execution. Whether designing digital products or immersive environments, my goal is always the same: to connect vision with execution and create experiences that feel thoughtful, grounded, and real.
